Our Story

Keller's newest morning tradition.

Kim's Donut is a family-run shop proud to call Keller home. We're here to share fresh donuts and sausage rolls with the community — traditional style, not drenched in icing, not too sweet.

We open at 5am and bake fresh every single day. Come early — by noon, we're almost out.
